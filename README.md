# Ajax Login
> Ajax Login is a must-have extension for any online Magento store. This module offers the best login experience for customers by allowing them to sign up and login to your system more quickly via a provided popup. 


* Allow customers to log in quickly using Ajax form
* Easy to customize popup design


## Features
   Overview

Some may wonder whether Ajax Login is too simple to support their online store. But they don’t know that the benefits this module brings will exceed their expectations. It does not stop at increasing the registration number of your site by improved user experience, but reducing the redirection for users and increasing customer retention as well.

With Ajax Login, the processes including Login, Retrieve Password and Registration are made simple and easy. After successfully installing Ajax Login, your customers can register, login or get their forgotten password directly on any page without any unexpected re-directions.


   Features List

* Allow customers to log in, retrieve password and register easily and quickly
* Provide an intuitive design and easy-to-run functionality for the popup
* Be applicable to all pages
* Enable/Disable extension in just one click
* Display a nice Ajax popup after customer click on Register or Login button
* Enable to redirect the the customer page on success
* Works well with Magento captcha
* Allows to display newsletter subscription in the popup
* Allows to display terms and conditions in the popup
* Support multiple stores
